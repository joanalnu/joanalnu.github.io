About Me

I'm a passionate student researcher with a particular interest in cosmology, learning to address the Hubble Tension, a long-standing challenge in the field related with dark energy, the mysterious force accelerating the expansion of the Universe. Furthermore, I created the CAPIBARA Collaboration, a group of high school students willing to study the high-energy cosmos in it's ionic and photonic states. Additionally, I'm also a skilled programmer, demonstrated through work such as the Gen10 project and this website, which I coded from scratch. Beyond research, I enjoy playing the piano, both solo and collaboratively, as well as volunteering at Barcelona's Science Museum CosmoCaixa and with the NGO Cycling Without Age.
