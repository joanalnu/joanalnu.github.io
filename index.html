About Me

I'm a Physics BSc student at LMU Munich and a passionate student researcher. I'm particularly interested in cosmology and the challenge of dark energy, the mysterious force accelerating the expansion of the Universe. In 2024, I created the CAPIBARA Collaboration, a group of high school students aiming to launch CubeSats to study the high-energy cosmos in its particle and light states. Additionally, I'm also a skilled programmer, demonstrated through work such as the Gen10 project and this website, which I coded from scratch.

News

Participated in GoPhysics! Workshop by Perimeter Institute

A series of asynchronous materials and two online discussions on gravity (general relativity) and black holes. We also got to chat with PI researchers and ask them questions about their job! Image by Perimeter Institute.

International Research Stay on Multi-Messenger Cosmology

Completed a 5-week research stay at the Osservatorio Astronomico di Brera, Sede di Merate (OAB-INAF) within the High Energy Astrophysics Group (HEA) on multi-messenger cosmology with the Epeak-Eiso relation and distance moduli from a simulated populations BNS mergers detectable by Einstein Telescope.

Graduated top-of-the class from Deutsche Schule Barcelona

Graduated with 1.0/1.0 Abitur obtaining both German and Spanish High School Diplomas. Attended virtually the graduation ceremony since I was in Hamburg at the time.

Participant at Bundeswettbewerb Jugend Forscht in Hamburg

Participated in the final round of the Jugend Forscht science Competition. Over 10,000 young people participate, but only around 200 reach the final round, where I was amazed to learn about so many interesting projects and people!

Attended STARMUS La Palma 2025

Attended the STARMUS Festival in La Palma, featuring lectures from Nobel Prize winners, astronauts, renowned scientists and the iconic Jane Goodall. The event was inspiring, highlighting the importance of pursuing one's passions.

1st Prize at JuFo Landeswettbewerb NRW

Won the first prize and a special prize of scientific photography at Jugend Forscht Nordrhein-Westfalen 2025, presenting my project on SNIa for cosmological distance measurements and the Hubble-Lemaître constant. Looking forward to participate in the final round (Bundeswettbewerb) in Hamburg!

Presentation at CosmoCaixa Science Museum
