PERFORMANCE NOTES

Setup & Recording

Performances recorded on a Yamaha Digital Piano P-45 and produced with GarageBand. Video recordings made at the Colegio Alemán de Barcelona.

This collection reflects my exploration of diverse musical traditions—from jazz standards and classical arrangements to contemporary film scores. Each piece represents both technical study and artistic interpretation.
