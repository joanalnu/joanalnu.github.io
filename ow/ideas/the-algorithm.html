Thought 2

the algorithm

How much of your life does the algorithm control?

From the music we hear, to the videos we watch. The news we read, social media we consume, posts we scroll.

The opinions we get to know and the stories we listen to.

The work we do, the road we go.

What we get to experience or,

in other words, the life we live.

  • What is Music to spotify?
  • What is Speech to twitter?
  • What are Friends to facebook?
  • What is Beauty to instagram?
  • What is Passion to linkedin?
  • What is Fast to google maps?

Maybe the fastest way is the one where you get to know that person.

The one where you discover your passion,

the one where you get lost.

Lost in time and lost in space, but pencil sharp on your thoughts.

Or maybe simply the one where you aren't hit by a drunk driver.

You must reclaim your limited amoun of time on Earth. And your time begins, now.
