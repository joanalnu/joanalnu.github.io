I am a B.Sc. Physics student at LMU Munich working at the intersections of high-energy astrophysics, cosmology, and relativistic foundations[cite: 7, 17, 18]. I lean towards building things openly—whether analyzing transient fields with code or establishing student networks like the CAPIBARA Collaboration[cite: 20, 26, 27, 30]. When away from an editor, I am typically mapping out thoughts in my ideas directory or making music.

Constrained cosmological density parameters (Ωm, ΩΛ) via χ2 minimization of empirical Epeak–Eiso relations[cite: 20]. Explored synergies with simulated binary neutron star merger populations from Einstein Telescope expectations[cite: 20].
