Problema 02

The Geometry Challenge

You are a detective on the case of a missing architect. The only clue left is a single piece of paper. On one side there is a drawing of a house like below (not to scale), 

   /\ 
  /  \ 
 /____\ 
 |\  /| 
 | \/ | 
 | /\ | 
 |/__\|

on the reverse there is a note

"The key to my disappearing is saved in a Swiss bank. Understand the geometry of this house to obtain the id number and the password of this safe."

Let the side of the square be $a = 6\text{ cm}$. To obtain the password, compute the following and stack them one after each other (e.g., if the results are $1$, $32$, $5$; the password would be 1325):

  1. Perimeter of the square
  2. Perimeter of the hat triangle (Assume height $= 4\text{ cm}$)
  3. Perimeter of the house
  4. Area of the square
  5. Area of 1 of the 4 triangles inside the square
  6. Area of the house
  7. Volume of the cube with the same side ($6\text{ cm}$)

To obtain the safe number, you need to compute 2 numbers. Imagine that the square part of the house is enclosed by a circle, each of the 4 vertices of the square touching the circle (the diameter is equivalent to the diagonal length). Compute, rounding to the nearest whole integer:

  • Perimeter (Circumference) of the circle
  • Area of the circle

    • What are the password and id number?

    Solution Key

    1. Password Calculation:

    • Perimeter of the square: $P_{\text{square}} = 4 \times 6 = 24\text{ cm}$
    • Perimeter of the hat triangle: The base is $6\text{ cm}$. With a height of $4\text{ cm}$, the two roof sides form $3\text{-}4\text{-}5$ right triangles. Slant sides $= \sqrt{3^2 + 4^2} = 5\text{ cm}$. $P_{\text{triangle}} = 6 + 5 + 5 = 16\text{ cm}$.
    • Perimeter of the house: 3 sides of the square + 2 slant sides of the roof $= (3 \times 6) + (2 \times 5) = 28\text{ cm}$.
    • Area of the square: $A_{\text{square}} = 6^2 = 36\text{ cm}^2$.
    • Area of 1 inner triangle: The square is divided evenly into 4 triangles by its diagonals. $A_{\text{inner}} = \frac{36}{4} = 9\text{ cm}^2$.
    • Area of the house: Area of the square + Area of the roof triangle $= 36 + (\frac{1}{2} \times 6 \times 4) = 36 + 12 = 48\text{ cm}^2$.
    • Volume of the cube: $V = 6^3 = 216\text{ cm}^3$.

    Password String: 24162836948216

    2. Safe ID Number Calculation:

    The diagonal of the square is $d = 6\sqrt{2}\text{ cm}$, which acts as the diameter of the circumscribed circle. Thus, the radius is $r = 3\sqrt{2}\text{ cm}$.

    • Perimeter of the circle: $C = 2\pi r = 2 \times \pi \times 3\sqrt{2} = 6\sqrt{2}\pi \approx 26.66 \rightarrow \mathbf{27}$
    • Area of the circle: $A = \pi r^2 = \pi \times (3\sqrt{2})^2 = 18\pi \approx 56.55 \rightarrow \mathbf{57}$

    Safe ID Number: 2757
