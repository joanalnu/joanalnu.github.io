About Me

I am a passionate student researcher leading innovative projects like the CAPIBARA Collaboration, a high school initiative to explore the high-energy cosmos, both its ionic and photonic sides. My research focuses on cosmology, particularly addressing the Hubble Tension, a long-standing challenge in the field, and studying dark energy, the mysterious force accelerating the expansion of the Universe. I am also a skilled programmer and data analyst, demonstrated through work such as the Gen10 project, the TdM game, and this website, which I coded from scratch. Beyond research, I enjoy playing the piano, both solo and collaboratively, as well as volunteering at Barcelona’s Science Museum CosmoCaixa and with the NGO Cycling Without Age.
