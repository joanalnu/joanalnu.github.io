About Genetics10

Genetics10 is a Python-based tool designed for students and educators to explore genetics and biology concepts, including DNA transcription, mutations, protein structures, and gene editing. It is built using Jupyter Notebook and requires no installation, making it accessible and classroom-friendly. The tool is open-source, scalable, and customizable to fit varying levels of expertise.

In addition, the GEN-API enables integration of Genetics10 functionalities directly into your projects, making it ideal for research and web applications.

Explore Genetics10 on GitHub

Key Features

  • DNA to RNA transcription and amino acid sequencing
  • Simulate genetic mutations
  • Compare DNA sequences
  • Easy iteration through datasets
  • Visualize protein structures using AlphaFold API integration
  • Gene editing simulating CRISPR-Cas9

GEN-API

The GEN-API provides programmatic access to Genetics10’s features, allowing integration into web apps, research projects, or educational programs. Enhance your coding projects with powerful genetics tools directly at your fingertips.
