About Genetics10

Gen10 is a Python package designed for students to explore genetics and biology concepts. This package provides useful functions ready-to-use for students to focus on the learning.

It requires any software or app installation, making it accessible and classroom-friendly, perfect for school managed devices.

In short, Gen10 provides the genomics functions you need right at your fingertips.

Explore Gen10 on GitHub

Key Features

DNA/RNA Transcription

Convert between DNA and RNA with amino acid sequencing

Genetic Mutations

Simulate random or specific site mutations

Sequence Comparison

Compare DNA sequences and find complementaries

GC Content

Calculate GC content and melting temperatures

Protein Visualization

Visualize protein structures with AlphaFold

CRISPR Simulation

Simulate gene editing with CRISPR-Cas9
