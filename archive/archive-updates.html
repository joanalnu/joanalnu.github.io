Delivered keynote presentation titled "El Universo: Cosmología, Gravitación y Astrofísica de Altas Energías" at educational institution, discussing contemporary topics in cosmology and gravitational physics.

Research Stay at Institute of Space Sciences (ICE-CSIC-IEEC)

Cepheid Variable Stars Research Project
