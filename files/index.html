Hi! I'm a B.Sc. Physics student at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität (LMU) with a deep interest in high-energy astrophysics, cosmology, and relativistic foundations. Beyond my academic coursework, I love bringing people together to explore science—whether that is founding the CAPIBARA Collaboration for students or doing science outreach.

When I'm not writing Python scripts to analyze supernovae or gamma-ray bursts, you can usually find me compiling new thoughts in my ideas folder or playing music.

Selected Research & Projects

GRB Cosmography & Relativistic Foundations
Osservatorio Astronomico di Brera (OAB-INAF), Italy
June — July 2025
Constrained cosmological density parameters (Ωm, ΩΛ) via χ2 minimization of empirical Epeak–Eiso relation and explored synergies with simulated binary neutron star merger populations from Einstein Telescope expectations.

Leading a team of students studying the scientific motivation and requirements for a student-led γ-ray transient observing CubeSat mission. I founded this collaboration to help high school and university students learn astrophysics together.

Institute of Space Sciences (ICE-CSIC-IEEC), Spain
June — July 2024
